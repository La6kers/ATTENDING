## 9.1 Visual AI Diagnostics

### The Revolution: AI That Sees What Humans Miss

**Capabilities:**
- Dermatology: Skin lesion analysis, melanoma detection
- Wound Assessment: Healing progress, infection risk
- Ophthalmology: Diabetic retinopathy screening
- Point-of-Care: Throat, ear, nail analysis
- Document OCR: Extract data from uploaded records

## 9.2 Care Coordination Hub

### The Revolution: Breaking Down Silos

**Capabilities:**
- E-Consults: Async specialist consultations
- Care Team Chat: Multi-provider communication
- Shared Care Plans: Collaborative treatment planning
- Handoff Management: Safe transitions of care
- External Provider Network: Cross-organization collaboration

### Impact

| Current State | With Care Hub |
|---------------|---------------|
| Phone tag for referrals | Instant e-consult |
| Faxed records | Shared digital workspace |
| Fragmented care plans | Unified treatment view |
| Lost handoffs | Tracked transitions |
